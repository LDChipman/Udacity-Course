Teemo

Teemo is the literal devil.

  • Blinding Dart: Teemo shoots a dart at someone blinding them.
  • Move Quick: Teemo gains 52% bonus movement speed.
  • Toxic Shot: Teemo's attacks poison the target dealing damage over time
  • Noxious Trap: Teemo tosses a mushroom trap on the ground which turns invisible after 1 second. If someone steps on it a cloud of poison sprays up in a 10 foot area, damging and poisoning everyone in it.

Teemo is a yordle from bandle city. He is a scout. If you encounter him in the wild. Run.
